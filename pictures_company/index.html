Our company

We are a company that is dedicated to the field of photography in a personalized way, where we offer that your memories are recorded, and you can edit them to your liking. At Pictures Company we work to improve every day, every photograph we take is a story and a lesson. For us, it is a pleasure that our clients choose us and give us their trust. Being part of their moments is one of the reasons why we do this work with love and dedication. Our professionals will leave your memory engraved in different editions and photographic formats.

Our story

Over time children grow and adults change, every opportunity deserves to be remembered! You can schedule a date in advance, contacting us via email or by phone, according to your convenience. History of the company: Pictures Company was founded in 2010 by Matías Schvindt, an amateur photographer, in the City of Buenos Aires, Argentina. From a very young age, Matías was interested in the visual arts and was fascinated by photography. His grandfather Roberto gave him his first camera at the age of 13, that was the beginning of his adventure, which has been going on for many years and experiences. Today he works with a team of professionals.
