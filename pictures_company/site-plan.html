Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to show visitors the work we offer to clients and the many ways you can be creative. The goal is to reach more people with the service of a professional photographer and create unforgettable memories, as they say, "a picture says more than a thousand words."

Audience

The site is aimed at those people who seek to immortalize moments, memories and experiences in photographs taken by a professional, and in it, they will be able to find examples, previous projects, as well as customer opinions.

Home page

Make a memorable keepsake of your special moment! We are a company that is dedicated to the field of photography in a personalized way, where we offer that your memories are recorded, and you can edit them to your liking. At Pictures Company we work to improve every day, every photograph we take is a story and a lesson. For us, it is a pleasure that our clients choose us and give us their trust. Being part of their moments is one of the reasons why we do this work with love and dedication. Our professionals will leave your memory engraved in different editions and photographic formats. Over time children grow and adults change, every opportunity deserves to be remembered! You can schedule a date in advance, contacting us via email or by phone, according to your convenience. History of the company: Pictures Company was founded in 2010 by Matías Schvindt, an amateur photographer, in the City of Buenos Aires, Argentina. From a very young age, Matías was interested in the visual arts and was fascinated by photography. His grandfather Roberto gave him his first camera at the age of 13, that was the beginning of his adventure, which has been going on for many years and experiences. Today he works with a team of professionals.
